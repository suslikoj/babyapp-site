About the app

Baby w/o allergies is a practical helper for the days when it’s too much: eczema, reaction, uncertainty, fatigue.

Overview

Everything in one place: logs, guides, allergens and recipes.

Structure

Guidance step by step. Less guessing, more confidence in decisions.

Whole-picture view

Finding real causes across allergens, environment, diet and mental well-being.

Trust

Verified information explained clearly, without fear-based messaging.

Who it’s for

Parent of a small child with signs of allergy

For parents of children under 2 years old with eczema or other signs of food intolerance. When you are trying to understand what is happening and are looking for a system instead of trial and error.

Parent in the elimination phase

For those who are currently going through an elimination diet and need structure, overview and certainty that they are moving in the right direction step by step.

Parent at their wits' end

For those who have already tried the diet or have gone through part of the process and are now stuck. The application will help you establish, evaluate the results and continue meaningfully.
