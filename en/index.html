Do you feel unsure what is triggering your child’s symptoms?

  • The eczema keeps getting worse and you don’t know why.
  • Every new food feels stressful.
  • You try one thing after another without clear answers.
  • You need a system that keeps the whole process together.

A clear system instead of chaos

Bejby bez alergií helps parents track foods, symptoms and reactions in context. It guides you from the elimination phase to gradual allergen testing, making the whole process clearer and easier to manage.

Hi, I’m Jiřina

I’m a mum of a child with multiple allergies. I went through a period when I didn’t know what was triggering the symptoms – eczema, reactions, endless trying and uncertainty.

That’s why I created Baby without allergies, an app that helps parents manage difficult situations connected with eczema and food allergies in babies and children.

I wanted to create something parents can always have at hand and use as long-term support. I’m a nutrition consultant and a programming enthusiast.

I hope the app helps you as much as it helped me.
