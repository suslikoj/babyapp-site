About the app

Baby w/o allergies is a practical helper for the days when it’s too much: food, reactions, uncertainty, exhaustion.

Overview

Everything in one place: logs, guides, allergens and recipes.

Structure

Guidance step by step. Less guessing, more confidence in decisions.

Whole-picture view

Finding real causes across allergens, environment, diet and mental well-being.

Trust

Verified information explained clearly, without fear-based messaging.

How it works

A simple system that keeps the whole process together.

  1. 1

    Start & setup

    A personalized start based on your situation.

  2. 2

    Elimination phase

    A clear plan and daily steps — no improvising.

  3. 3

    Guided allergen testing

    Step-by-step reintroduction with reminders.

  4. 4

    Recipes

    Recipes with automatic filtering by diet phase or allergies.

  5. 5

    Symptom calendar

    Track reactions, eczema and notes over time.

  6. 6

    Summaries & results

    Connections in one place — easy to share with a professional.
