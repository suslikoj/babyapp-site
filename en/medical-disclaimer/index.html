1. Purpose of the App

The BabyApp application serves as an informational and supportive tool for parents of children with food allergies or suspected food allergies.

The content of the application, including recommendations, articles, recipes, and suggested approaches to introducing foods, is provided solely for informational and educational purposes.

2. What the App Is Not

The application does not provide medical services, is not a medical device, and does not replace medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional healthcare.

3. Individual Differences

Food reactions and dietary responses may vary significantly between children. Therefore, information provided in the application should not be considered universal medical advice.

4. Consult a Professional

Before making any changes to a child’s diet, in case of suspected allergic reactions, or in case of any health concerns, users should always consult a physician or qualified healthcare professional.

5. User Responsibility

By using the application, users acknowledge that all decisions regarding healthcare, nutrition, or dietary measures are made at their own responsibility.
