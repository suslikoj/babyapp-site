2. Nature of the Application

BabyApp is a digital tool intended mainly for parents of children with food allergies or suspected food allergies.

The application is primarily used for:

  • recording information about foods and reactions
  • tracking dietary regimens
  • recording food testing progress
  • providing informational articles and recipes
  • general recommendations related to food introduction

The application is intended mainly for children up to 2 years of age.

Food reactions are individual and may vary for each child. In case of health issues, suspected allergic reaction, or worsening of the child’s condition, a physician or medical specialist must be contacted immediately.
